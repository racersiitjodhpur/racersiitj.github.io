Specialization

Our team is divided in such a way that all members get hands-on experience to apply their classroom knowledge into practice. Students also get a chance to hone their soft skills through fund raising, material sourcing and team management. But most of all, we learn to work efficiently as a team and complete the task at hand. We have divided ourselves into the following sub-teams:

Braking

Assemble a reliable braking system for driver safety

Design & FEA

Design a light, compact and sturdy chassis supporting all subsystems

Electrical

Overlook entire manufacturing phase along with advanced manufacturing techniques and performs regular checks on overall built quality

Suspension and Steering

Responsible for maintaining vehicle stability and ride comfort in addition to a responsive steering system to provide minimum turning radius

Marketing & Media

Reach out to potential sponsors and handles overall media impact of the team and its sponsors

Transmission

Design a robust power-train assembly which satisfies performance requirements of the vehicle
