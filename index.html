    A Brief History

    Team Racers IITJ was formed in 2012, after four years of inception of the institute. In February 2014, the team participated in its first main event in New Delhi, placing 31st out of 100 teams and completely shattering any and all expectations. With 25 returning members and one year of valuable experience under their belts, the team competed at the event at Indore, in February 2016, placing 63rd overall. In February 2017, Racers IITJ competed again in Indore and finished 70th out of 107 teams. With similar record in 2018, Team Racers IITJ will return to Indore, Madhya Pradesh with the hopes of a top-25 finish at the BAJA SAE India 2019

    About Us

    We are a student-run competition team given the challenge of designing, building, promoting and racing a single seat prototype offroad vehicle. It is our job to interpret the challenge given the constraints in any way we choose and implement our ideas to finally bring out an all-terrain vehicle. But basically we are a bunch of automobile enthusiasts comprising of undergraduate engineering students from all four years following our passion, constantly burning in the crucible of competition

    Our Mission

    To gain real-world experience in engineering design, manufacturing, and marketing through the production of a single-seat, off-road racing vehicle by bring together those brains which are diligently working on a myriad of inventive technologies.

    Our Plan

    To control every aspect of the process by handling design, fabrication, team management, and marketing and thus progress from the conceptual design phase to a completely functional vehicle both effectively and efficiently.

    Our Vision

    To give our best in the virtual round of Mahindra SAE BAJA 2018 through which we can make our dream to build the best ever All Terrain Vehicle come true in the main-event of SAE BAJA 2019 with the help of our team members.

    Specialization

    Our team is divided in such a way that all members get hands-on experience to apply their classroom knowledge into practice. Students also get a chance to hone their soft skills through fund raising, material sourcing and team management. But most of all, we learn to work efficiently as a team and complete the task at hand. We have divided ourselves into the following sub-teams:

    Braking

    Assemble a reliable braking system for driver safety

    Design & FEA

    Design a light, compact and sturdy chassis supporting all subsystems

    Manufacturing & Quality

    Overlook entire manufacturing phase along with advanced manufacturing techniques and performs regular checks on overall built quality

    Marketing & Media

    Reach out to potential sponsors and handles overall media impact of the team and its sponsors

    Suspension and Steering

    Responsible for maintaining vehicle stability and ride comfort in addition to a responsive steering system to provide minimum turning radius

    Transmission

    Design a robust power-train assembly which satisfies performance requirements of the vehicle
